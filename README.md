# Aurora - AI-Powered Business Intelligence Platform

**ผู้ประกอบการส่วนใหญ่ไม่ได้ขาด Data แต่ขาดการเปลี่ยน Data ให้เป็น Action**

Aurora is a comprehensive Business Intelligence and Organizer platform designed specifically for SME business owners. We transform data from multiple sales channels into actionable insights, helping you make better business decisions faster.

## 🎯 Target Audience

SME business owners who:
- Conduct business through online or offline channels (Omnichannel)
- Use platforms like Facebook, TikTok, LINE OA, Shopee, Lazada
- Want to build their brand on social media
- Need help turning data into profitable actions

## 💡 Core Problems We Solve

### Pain 1: Data to Action Gap
**Problem:** Business owners have dashboards full of data (Facebook Ads, Google Analytics) but don't know what to do with it.

**Solution:** AI Decision Layer
- Pulls data from multiple sources (Facebook, Google, Shopee, Lazada)
- Cleans and processes data automatically
- Calculates key business metrics (ROAS, Conversion Rate, LTV, CAC)
- AI analyzes and provides recommendations in plain language
- **Explains every decision** with Pros & Cons before execution
- Hidden Template Prompt Engineering for optimal results

### Pain 2: Can't Keep Up with Market Trends
**Problem:** Sellers track trends slowly or not at all, missing profit opportunities.

**Solution:** Market Scout
- **Mode 1 - Trend Discovery:** AI analyzes Google Trends + Shopee/Lazada sales data to show trending products with margin estimates
- **Mode 2 - Value Validation:** Upload product images from markets/fairs and AI validates if Thai market will accept it

### Pain 3: Know What to Do, But Can't Execute
**Problem:** Store owners understand what should be done but lack time or execution capability.

**Solution:** Automation Center
- AI validates and notifies before automated actions
- Permission-based automation mechanism
- Helps bridge the Insight → Execution Gap

## 🔑 Killer Feature: "Explain Before Execute"

Every recommendation includes:
1. **Clear recommendation** with reasoning
2. **Detailed Pros (ข้อดี)** - Why this is good for your business
3. **Detailed Cons (ข้อเสีย)** - Risks and considerations
4. **Explanation (คำอธิบาย)** - How we arrived at this decision
5. **Confirmation** - You approve before we execute

This "Decision Transparency" builds trust and helps you understand your business better.
